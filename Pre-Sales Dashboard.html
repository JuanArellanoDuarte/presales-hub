How our outbound model works

A structured, disciplined approach to pipeline generation

What is a sequence?

A sequence is a curated list of ICP-aligned contacts enrolled in a structured 10-week outreach cadence. Each sequence is purpose-built for a specific seller's target accounts or an independent hunting motion like Procurement or Lost Opps.

What each sequence contains

Every week, for each contact in the sequence, the BDR executes three touches:

1 personalized email1 LinkedIn touchpoint1 outbound call

That's 10 emails, 10 LinkedIn touchpoints, and 10 calls per contact over the course of the sequence.

How BDRs structure their week

Each BDR runs a maximum of 2 active sequences simultaneously. Two days per week are dedicated exclusively to sequence work — one day for emails and LinkedIn, another for calls. The remaining three days are reserved for Digital, Events, and S7 Ops pipeline nurturing, alongside daily inbound qualification and NDA handling.

When a meeting gets booked

The seller is always the first to know. The BDR creates the opportunity in Stage 7 in Salesforce. If the meeting outcome is positive, the seller accepts and the BDR moves it to Stage 6. If not conclusive, the BDR continues nurturing in Stage 7.

What happens at week 10

If no meeting is booked after 10 weeks, the full contact list is handed to Growth Marketing for enrollment in the Always-On campaign.
